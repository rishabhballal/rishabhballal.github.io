I am interested in the application of microlocal analysis to quantum field theory. A fundamental result in this sector is the microlocal spectrum condition, which ties the regularity of quantum correlations to the causal structure of the spacetime. It forms the backbone of renormalisation and even has implications in entanglement.


My master's thesis investigated the fulfillment of the microlocal spectrum condition in non-commutative Minkowski spacetime. I am now trying to derive a lower bound on the quantum energy density in the same setting.
